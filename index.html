Operating method

How I promote affiliate offers

  1. Offer research Review the product, audience, sales page, partner resources, payout, approval requirements, and policy risk.
  2. Landing page build Create a review, comparison, guide, or quiz page with clear disclosure and practical decision criteria.
  3. Controlled paid search Launch only after human review, using low-budget Search campaigns with phrase and exact match keywords.
  4. Measurement and iteration Track outbound clicks and sales where available, then adjust copy, keywords, and landing content from data.

Traffic strategy

Search traffic with intent, controls, and review gates.

Google Search first

Campaigns are planned around specific buyer-intent queries, comparison searches, and course research terms.

No broad-match launch

Initial testing avoids broad match, Display Network, and automated budget changes until performance is understood.

Negative keyword hygiene

Terms such as free, download, pirated, jobs, scam, and unrelated research intent are reviewed early.

Budget discipline

Campaigns start small, with loss limits, manual review, and no automated spend increases.

Compliance promise

Transparent promotion, brand-safe execution.

I do not use fake reviews, invented testimonials, cloaking, misleading redirects, unauthorized brand bidding, false scarcity, or guaranteed-result claims. Promotional content is written to help buyers make informed decisions.

  • Visible affiliate disclosure on landing pages.
  • No bidding on trademark terms unless explicitly allowed.
  • No use of brand names in domains that could imply impersonation.
  • No medical, financial, or transformation guarantees.
  • Human review before publishing ads or changing budgets.
  • Tracking and reporting used for optimization, not manipulation.
